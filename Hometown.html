Introduction

Cambodia

Cambodia is a country that is a part of Southeast Asia. Located inbetween Thailand and Vietnam. It's flag consists of 3 horizontal stripes and one temple representing Angkor wat which is the largest temple in the world

Phnom Penh

Phnom Penh is the capital city of Cambodia. It is the city where I were born. Phnom Penh is noted for its historical architecture and attractions. It became the national capital in 1434. Phnom Penh is also where I graduated highschool at Western International School in 2021.

Culture

Throughout Cambodia's long history, religion has been a major source of cultural inspiration. Over nearly three millennia, Cambodians have developed a unique Cambodian culture and belief system from the syncreticism of indigenous animistic beliefs and the Indian religions of Buddhism and Hinduism. Indian culture and civilization, including its languages and arts reached mainland Southeast Asia around the 1st century AD.

Cuisine

Due to the sustained historic interaction and shared influences, Cambodian cuisine has many similarities with its neighbouring Southeast Asian cuisines of Thailand, Laos, Vietnam, and Indonesia. Cambodian cuisine also uses fish sauce in soups, stir-fried cuisine, and as dippings.

Clothing

Clothing in Cambodia is one of the most important aspects of the culture. Cambodian fashion differs according to ethnic group and social class. Khmer people traditionally wear a checkered scarf called a Krama.
